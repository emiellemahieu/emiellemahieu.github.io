Emiel Lemahieu is a quantitative researcher at the Belgian robo-advisory InvestSuite and Ghent University. His research focuses on data-driven portfolio construction techniques. Starting from the general aim to offer smooth-ride investments to retail investors through robo-advisory tools, Emiel has explored traditional risk-based optimization techniques and ways to improve them using novel machine learning approaches. In particular, his research interests lie in using generative machine learning techniques for simulating optimal drawdown portfolios and graph-based methods to assess common sources of downside risk. Emiel holds an MSc Finance from Ghent University and King's College London and is currently pursuing his PhD at Ghent University.
